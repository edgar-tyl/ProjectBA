What is the total value of ownership transfers for each apartment? ||| SELECT AO.APARTMENTID , SUM( OT.PRICE ) AS TOTALVALUEOFTRANSFERS FROM APARTMENTOWNERSHIP AO JOIN OWNERSHIPTRANSFERS OT ON OT.TRANSFERID = AO.TRANSFERID GROUP BY AO.APARTMENTID ;

Which owner owns more than 6 apartment? ||| SELECT COUNT( AO.APARTMENTID ) AS NUMBEROFAPARTMENTS , O.OWNERNAME FROM OWNERS O JOIN APARTMENTOWNERSHIP AO ON O.OWNERID = AO.OWNERID GROUP BY O.OWNERNAME HAVING COUNT( AO.APARTMENTID ) > 6 ;
